Open call for 2 day workshop and a pop up exhibition
Deadline to apply the 6th of January


--!Schedule!--
1-2nd of February from 10:30 till 17:30
Bio-techno workshop - you will learn about electronics, circuits, programming and how it can intersect with art+science. You will develop small projects using materials provided. This is meant to be a hackathon (rapid and collaborative prototyping) and we will be looking at making fun projects, rather than finished pieces.
3rd of February at 19:00
Pop up exhibition - we will present what we made together in Filet gallery for one night.
--!What to expect!--
Every participant will get a robotics kit to take home, budget for their project + communal lunches, tea, coffee, snacks on both days. Accessibility needs will be met. ++ It is going to be a fun experience, you can meet people, learn something new and present your project.
--!Equipment!--
Participants are encouraged to bring their own computers, but a limited number of computers are available if needed. The rest will be provided.
--!Who!--
Anyone is welcome to apply. People who especially feel excluded from such spaces, opportunities and topics are encouraged to apply!
--!Where!--
FILET gallery, London
